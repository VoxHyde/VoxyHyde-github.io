Secret Singularity

A full 3D city race. You are a black hole dropped into a park-and-street district with benches, trees, buildings, lamps, poles, cars, and three rival bot singularities.

  • Move: `WASD` or arrow keys.
  • Eat: small city props first, then work your way up into cars and buildings.
  • Level: mass thresholds raise your level, but levels improve handling and pull instead of directly making you bigger.
  • Fight: if your radius is bigger than a rival bot, you can swallow it. If theirs is bigger, run.
  • View: tap `C` to cycle the camera angle.
  • Win: become the last black hole standing or finish the city while staying the biggest.
